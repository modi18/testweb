My Leadership Philosophy

Oldest of five; school captaincy; university mentoring. I lead by doing — integrity, empathy, and steady action.

Leadership began at home, long before titles. I learned to guide with patience and service, then carried that into peer mediation (Year 5), SRC captain (Year 6), and class captain/student voice roles through Years 8–10. Today, I step forward first so others feel safe to follow.
